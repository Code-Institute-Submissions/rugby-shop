HOW DO I GET MY ORDER DELIVERED TO A STORE?
(Please note Click & Collect is temporarily not an option due to store closures.)

Order online, avoid the shipping costs, and collect your items from any of our 52 stores nationwide, whenever and wherever is most convenient for you. It couldn’t be easier:

Step 1: Place your order online

As part of the checkout process you will be offered the choice of home delivery or collect in store. Choose your nearest store from the drop down lists and complete your order.

Step 2: Get Confirmation

You will receive a 'Ready to Collect' email to confirm that your order is available for collection. Please wait for this confirmation email before you come in to collect your order.

Step 3: Collect In-Store

Head down to your chosen store with a copy of your confirmation email and a valid form of ID (drivers licence or passport), and produce both at the main in-store till point. They’ll hand over your package and that’s it!

HOW LONG DOES STANDARD DELIVERY TAKE?
"Due to measures put in place to combat COVID-19 order processing and delivery will take longer than normal"

Standard delivery to Republic of Ireland at present is taking 1 to 7 working days. If your order does not meet these timings, it may be because your item(s) are being dispatched from a different location. In this case, we will contact you with an expected delivery date. Standard delivery to the UK, Mainland Europe, United States and the Rest of the World can take 3 to 9 working days. Home Gym Equipment Delivery in the Republic of Ireland (Keepfit Range) and International Delivery does take longer. Please refer to our delivery information above for more detail. We always endeavour to get your order to you as quick as possible!

HOW MUCH DOES DELIVERY COST?

Delivery to any Elverys Intersport store is free.* Standard delivery to anywhere in the Republic of Ireland is free on all orders over €30. Home Gym Equipment (Keepfit Range) and International Delivery charges vary. Please refer to our delivery charges above for more detail.

CAN I TRACK MY ORDER?

Once you order has been dispatched from our warehouse with An Post you can then track you order on www.anpost.ie using your Order Number or your Tracking Number.

I HAVEN’T RECEIVED ALL OF MY ORDER?

If you find that your order has only been partially delivered, this may be because other items are being dispatched from a different location or we were out of stock on part of your order. If we are out of stock we will contact you with an expected delivery date or offer a full refund. If your order is obviously incomplete, please email online@rugbyshop.ie or call 1850 123 123.
