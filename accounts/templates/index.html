About Me


Rugby Shop was established in 1999 and is the oldest rugby store in Ireland. Following a take over by Staunton Sports in 1998, Elverys Sports have expanded from a single store operation to fifty four stores nationwide. This coming together brought a wealth of experience and in-depth knowledge of the sports retail business that is reflected in our shops today. Today, we employ over 700 hundred people and are proud to be wholly Irish owned.
