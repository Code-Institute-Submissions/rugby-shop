About Us


Rugby Shop was established in 1999 and is the oldest rugby store in Ireland. Following a take over by Staunton Sports in 1998, Rugby Shop have expanded from a single store operation to fifty four stores nationwide. This coming together brought a wealth of experience and in-depth knowledge of the sports retail business that is reflected in our shops today. Today, we employ over 100 hundred people and are proud to be wholly Irish owned.

We cater for both the casual sporting community and top sports athletes. Our business has been built on a high level of customer service, quality sports goods, reliability and above all instilling confidence in the public that Rugby Shop is the retail store for those interested in sport.

As well as stocking the top brand names in sportswear, Rugby Shop also offer a vast range of own brand clothing, accessories and equipment, offering customers great value and top quality.

We aspire to be a leading sports and lifestyle retailer internationally and to deliver sustainable growth for our shareholders in the medium to long term by offering our customers an unrivalled range of high quality leading brands.
